## 4. Reel Scripts (film on phone, 15–30s each)

**A — "Packing your order" (the workhorse; reuse weekly)**
1. (2s) Order note/phone with WhatsApp order visible, name blurred
2. (3s) Empty basket placed on table, top-down
3. (3s) Items placed one by one — jump cut per item
4. (3s) Shred/filler fluffed in
5. (3s) Ribbon tied, slow-mo the pull
6. (2s) Finished hamper spin or slide-in of the H & A sticker
7. (2s) End card: logo + "Order on WhatsApp — link in bio"
Overlay text: "POV: you ordered a handmade hamper from a small Bengaluru business 💜"

**B — Wrapping ASMR (no talking)**
Close-up hands only: paper crease sounds, scissors gliding, ribbon curl. Natural audio, no music. Caption does the selling. These get shares/saves — the algorithm's favourite signals.

**C — Before/After**
1. (2s) Text: "They asked for a birthday hamper for their mom 🥹"
2. (4s) Flat lay of loose items
3. (1s) Hand snap / transition
4. (5s) Finished hamper, slow pan
5. (3s) End card: "Tell us the occasion. We'll make it special. — link in bio"

**D — "3 handmade gifts under ₹999"**
3s per product: Name Jar ₹599 → Candle Set ₹749 → Macramé ₹849, price as text overlay, upbeat trending audio, end card "all handmade in Bengaluru 🛍 link in bio". Listicle reels are the most-saved format in gifting.

**E — Festival last-call (adapt per festival)**
Fast montage (1.5s/shot) of best hampers + big text: "RAKHI IS IN 5 DAYS" → "we deliver across India" → "order by Aug 24" → logo. Urgency converts lurkers.

## 5. FAQ Posts (carousels; slide 1 = the question big, slides 2-4 = answer, last slide = CTA)

1. **"Can I customise a hamper?"** — Yes, everything: pick items, add names/dates/notes, set a budget and we curate. Example: the Custom Photo Memory Box (₹1,699) engraved with their photo. CTA: "WhatsApp us your occasion."
2. **"How does delivery work?"** — Pan-India shipping; free gift wrapping above ₹999; Bengaluru orders arrive fastest. Order 4–5 days early for festivals.
3. **"What's inside the hampers?"** — Walk through the Rustic Gift Hamper (₹1,299) item by item. Show, don't list.
4. **"Do you do bulk / corporate orders?"** — Yes: employee Diwali gifts, client hampers, wedding favours, custom branding. CTA: enquiry link (site's Corporate Gifting section).
5. **"Why handmade?"** — The story post. Mass-produced vs. made-for-you; your hands, your process. Ends with the maker photo. Pin this one too.

## 7. Weekly Routine (≈2 hrs/week total)

- **While packing real orders (0 extra min):** prop the phone up, film everything. One packing session = 2–3 reels of raw footage.
- **Sunday (45 min):** cut 2 reels in CapCut/IG editor, schedule the week's posts in the Meta Business Suite app (free, allows scheduling).
- **Daily (10 min):** post the story, reply to every comment and DM within a few hours — response speed feeds both the algorithm and sales.

**Track monthly (Instagram Insights):** followers, reach, saves, and — the one that pays — profile visits → link clicks.
